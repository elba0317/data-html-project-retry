HOW NEW YORK CITY HAS BEEN EFFECTED BY THE PANDEMIC

This graphic focuses on COVID-19 data specific to New York City from its onset in March 2020 to October 1, 2021. The information provides data on infections, deaths, vaccinations and PPE funding.

by Elba T. Rodriguez

October 2021

There have been 889,288 confirmed cases of COVID-19 infection to date.

The amount of people hospitalized in New York City due to COVID-19 is 122,582.

34,284 New Yorkers have COVID-19 listed as an attributor to their fatality on their death certificates.

Since the advent of the COVID vaccine in December 2020, an average of 84.8 percent of New Yorkers in the five boroughs that are 18 and older, are vaccinated.
